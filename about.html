Water Reducible Primer

PCS-12295 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Primer

A titanate-modified, rust-inhibitive, air-dry primer designed specifically for use over less-than-ideal surface preparation. The product contains non-toxic, corrosion-inhibitive pigments and is free of heavy metals.

PCS-12155 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Primer

A rust-inhibitive, general-purpose primer for both air-dry and force-cure applications. The product contains non-toxic, corrosion-inhibitive pigments and is free of heavy metals. It is V.O.C. compliant and has no flash point.

Water Reducible Enamel

PCS-12250 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Gloss Enamel

A water-reducible, high gloss enamel for interior or exterior use, has good color and gloss retention, and the ability to be applied either direct to metal or to previously-painted surfaces.

PCS-12210 (Hydro-Tech) Water Reducible Wheel Enamel

A water-reducible, high gloss enamel for interior or exterior use, has good color and gloss retention, and the ability to be applied either direct to metal or to previously-painted surfaces.

PCS-2250 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Enamel, Asphalt Modified

An asphalt modified water-reducible enamel for interior or exterior use, has good color and gloss retention, and the ability to be applied either direct to metal or to previously-painted surfaces.

Alkyd Enamels

PCS-13275 High Solids Acrylic Modified Gloss Enamel, HAPS Free

An acrylic modified, high solids, alkyd enamel. The acrylic modification of the coating enhances the gloss and color retention properties for improved exterior durability and service life.

PCS-9010 High Gloss Fast Dry Alkyd Enamel (Direct-to-Metal Industrial Enamel)

An extremely versatile alkyd enamel. It has been modified to give fast dry times without the problems of embrittlement and poor adhesion generally associated with fast dry products of this type.

PCS-10270 Acrylic Modified Gloss (Air Dry Alkyd) Enamel

An acrylic modified alkyd enamel. The acrylic modification of the coating improves the gloss and color retention properties for improved exterior durability and service life.

Alkyd Primers

PCS-13155 High Solids Air Dry (Corrosive Inhibitive) Primer

A rust-inhibitive, general-purpose primer for both air-dry and force-cure applications. The product contains non-toxic, corrosion-inhibitive pigments and is free of heavy metals.

PCS-13156 High Solids Air Dry (Corrosive Inhibitive) Primer

A rust-inhibitive, general-purpose primer for both air-dry and force-cure applications. The product contains non-toxic, corrosion-inhibitive pigments and is free of heavy metals.

PCS-13195 High Solids Air Dry (Corrosive Inhibitive) Primer, High Build, HAPS Free

A rust-inhibitive, general-purpose primer for both air-dry and force-cure applications. The product contains non-toxic, corrosion-inhibitive pigments and is free of heavy metals. This product is free of volatile organic components classified as HAPs (Hazardous Air Pollutants) by the USEPA.

Epoxies

PCS-821 Grip-Tite High Gloss Epoxy Floor Finish

A two-component, self-leveling, high solids, epoxy finish designed for floors and other concrete surfaces combining extremely good abrasion and chemical resistance with both aesthetic and durable properties. Grip-Tite may be applied to damp surfaces if any liquid water is first removed; it contains a special solvent blend to facilitate the displacement of moisture contained in pores and crevices.

PCS-850 Polyamide (High Build) Epoxy Finish Coat

A VOC compliant, two component, cross-linked epoxy composition. It is designed to be used as a finish coat on steel, concrete and masonry surfaces in conjunction with various Permite primers.

PCS-895 (Glass Filled) Novolac Epoxy Coating

A two component glass filled novolac coating for highly corrosive environments, designed for application where the presence of solvents is not permitted.

PCS-800 (High Build) Polyamide Epoxy Primer

A VOC compliant, two component, cross-linked epoxy composition, designed to be used as a primer on steel, concrete and masonry surfaces in conjunction with various Permite finish coats.

PCS-803 Grip-Tite Penetrating Epoxy Primer

A VOC compliant, catalyzed epoxy composition designed to be used as a primer on steel, concrete and masonry surfaces in conjunction with various Permite epoxy toppings, floor coatings and topcoats.

PCS-809 100% Solids (Chem-Rest) Epoxy Resin for Mortars and Grouts

A quartz filled epoxy-phenolic composite complex designed for concrete restoration and/or protection where extreme chemical and abrasion resistance is necessary.

PCS-9043 (Chem-Rest) Permox Wet Process Epoxy

A glass filled epoxy coating that can be applied to wet surfaces when the ambient temperature is as low as 35o F. It is intended primarily for maintenance use on steel and concrete but is suitable for immersion service in some applications

PCS-9043 Type II Permox Glass-Flake Filled Epoxy Pipe Coating

A high-build glass-flake epoxy coating specifically designed for use on concrete, ductile iron, steel pipe and related equipment. It is self priming and epoxy modified to produce thick films up to 50 mils dry film thickness in a single coat, where required.

Polyurethanes

PCS-566 Polycote Polyester Urethane

A two-part polyester aliphatic polyurethane coating. Polyester is used rather than acrylic for increased chemical resistance. It has outstanding resistance to chemicals and solvents as well as excellent gloss and color retention on outdoor exposure.

PCS-504 Acrylic Polyurethane Finish

A VOC compliant, two component, aliphatic acrylic urethane, with good color and gloss retention designed to be used as a finish coat on steel, concrete and masonry surfaces in conjunction with various Permite Primers.

PCS-571 Polycote Acrylic Polyurethane (Automotive Grade)

A unique acrylic polyurethane based on an aliphatic urethane prepolymer. Polycote can be applied as received with both conventional and airless spray equipment as well as by brush or roller.

Aluminums

PCS-1100 & PCS-1100-40 High Heat Aluminum Paint

A ready-to-use leafing aluminum paint intended primarily for exterior/interior hot surfaces. Normally two thin coats provide adequate protection. Do not use a primer under PCS-1100. The material is self-priming but not recommended for highly corrosive environments

PCS-1902 Silicone High Heat Aluminum

An aluminum pigmented silicone resin finish coat for interior or exterior use where high temperatures are encountered. PCS-1902 has excellent weather and thermal shock resistance; wide temperature fluctuations do not affect the finish.

PCS-4050 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Gloss Enamel

A durable quick dry aluminum finish for moderately corrosive environments. PCS-4050 is designed to yield a dry spray within 15 feet of application. However, temperature and humidity can cause variations in distance.

PCS-5025 Hydro-Tech Water Reducible Gloss Enamel

A ready mixed leafing aluminum finish made with a polyurethane modified linseed oil resin. It has exceptional adhesion, flexibility, brilliance, exterior durability and hiding power; will not crack or peel due to wide temperature fluctuations.

Water Reducible Acrylics

PCS-4200 Waterborne Gloss Acrylic Finish

A water based acrylic enamel. It has excellent color and gloss retention.

Permox

PERMOX-CTF Coal-Tar Free Ceramic Filled Novolac Epoxy Lining

Permox CTF™ has the largest installation for an Environmentally “Preferable” domestic sewer service ranging from forcemains and gravity lines to pump stations. Since 2008 approximately one million linear feet of ductile iron pipe has been lined and installed with Permox CTF™. Along with being approved and used by all domestic ductile iron pipe manufacturers, thousands of fittings have been lined with Permox CTF™.
